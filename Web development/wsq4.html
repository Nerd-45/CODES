CRICKET WORLD CUP 2011

 The final was played on 2 April between India and 
Sri Lanka at Wankhede Stadium, Mumbai. India were crowned
 champions after winning by six wickets with only 10 balls
 remaining. India captain MS Dhoni was named man of the 
 match after an unbeaten, match-winning innings of 
 91 runs off 79 balls, including the final shot that
won the game. After the match, the Indian players
paid tribute to Sachin Tendulkar, who was playing 
in his last World Cup. The final had a viewership
 of about 997 million people all over the world.
